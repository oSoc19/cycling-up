Total amount of Villo! Rentals

Villo! Is a bike sharing scheme run in the Brussels Capital Region. Villo! Wants to improve and encourage sustainable mobility for Brussels citizens. Bicycles can be rented from 364 stations. The graph shows the total number of Villo! rentals from 2010 to 2017. The steady increase in this period is related to increased cycling as well as to the rise of the number of available bicycle stations.
