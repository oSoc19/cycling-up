How many people ride their bike to work?

Source: SPF Mobilité et Transports (Diagnostic des déplacements domicile-travail)

Citizens using bikes to commute to work within Brussels city increased from 2005 (1.2%) till 2017 (4.4%). This rise shows an augmentation in the demand for sustainable solutions when moving from home to work. Hence, the melioration of bikes infrastructures within the Brussels region enhance this steady increase.
